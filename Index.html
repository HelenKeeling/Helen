Hello and welcome

I’m Helen and I'm an aspiring web developer and amateur photographer.

I’ve created this site to log my progress as I start my journey in web development and to show some of my images to the world.

My parents bought our first computer when I was 13 years old and this was the first time I started dabbling in HTML and CSS. I loved that computer (even though it was running Windows ME) and wanted to learn more about the world of technology. I would spend my spare time researching and would soon help out family/friends/neighbours if they ever had computer trouble. I have to say my favorite thing to do was making projects for a website Girland.com (shout out to anyone who remembers it). Unfortunately like the website, all my code has gone which is sad as I'd like to have a look at all that now.

I decided to take computing at GCSE and then continue this on into college and eventually university. I graduated from the University of Derby in 2011 with a BSc (Hons) Information Technology but unfortunately I found finding a job within IT difficult and ended up going into Insurance after graduating.

After spending 6 years working within the Insurance industry I decided I wanted to go back into what I love which is web development. So here I am back at the beginning but determine to make it.
