Hello & Welcome

My name is Helen and I am a junior developer currently living and working in Nottingham, UK.

I have always had a passion for technology and web development which drove me to achieving a BSc (Hons) in Information Technology from the University of Derby.

Upon graduating life took a different turn and I started within the Insurance industry which is where I have been for the last 7 years. Since late 2018 my interest in web development was kickstarted and so this website was created to represent my progress as I start my journey back into web development.
